Welcome to Madhya Pradesh

Madhya Pradesh, often called the "Heart of India," is a state located in central India. Known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and natural beauty, it is home to ancient temples, palaces, and wildlife sanctuaries. Famous attractions include the UNESCO World Heritage Sites of Khajuraho Temples and Sanchi Stupa, as well as the vibrant city of Indore and the historical city of Gwalior.
