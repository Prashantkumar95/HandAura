Delivery Policy

Learn about our shipping methods, delivery charges, and how we handle your orders.

Shipping Policy

We strive to deliver your handmade products as quickly as possible. All orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Our delivery options include:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 1-3 business days
  • International Shipping: 7-14 business days (depending on destination)

Delivery Charges

We offer free delivery on all orders over ₹500 within India. For orders under ₹500, a delivery charge of ₹50 will be added. International shipping charges vary based on the location and weight of the product.

  • Free delivery on orders above ₹500 (India)
  • Flat ₹50 shipping charge for orders below ₹500 (India)
  • International shipping costs calculated at checkout

Order Tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can use this number to track your order at any time.

For more details, visit our Order Tracking Page.

FAQs

Here are some common questions we receive about delivery:

  • How long will my order take to arrive? - Orders typically arrive within 3-7 business days (India) or 7-14 days (International), depending on your location.
  • Can I change my delivery address after placing an order? - Please contact us immediately if you need to change your address before your order has been dispatched.
  • What should I do if my order is delayed? - Please contact our customer support team at support@handaura.com.
