Welcome to Assam

Assam, located in northeastern India, is known for its rich cultural heritage, lush green landscapes, and vibrant biodiversity. The state is famous for its tea plantations, particularly in regions like Jorhat and Dibrugarh, and is the world's largest producer of tea. Assam is also home to the Kaziranga National Park, a UNESCO World Heritage Site that houses the famous one-horned rhinoceros.
