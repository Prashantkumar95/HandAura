Welcome to Chhattisgarh

Bihar, located in eastern India, is known for its rich history, culture, and contribution to ancient Indian civilization. Its capital is Patna, situated along the Ganges River. Bihar is famous for being the birthplace of Buddhism and Jainism, with significant sites like Bodh Gaya and Nalanda. The state has a predominantly agriculture-based economy, growing crops like rice, wheat, and maize. Bhojpuri, Maithili, and Magahi are widely spoken.
