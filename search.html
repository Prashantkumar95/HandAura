Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh is known for its rich cultural heritage and authentic handmade art like Kalamkari paintings and Bidriware, which have been passed down through generations.

Bihar

Bihar is famous for Madhubani paintings and traditional Mithila art, which depict vibrant, nature-inspired motifs that are unique to the region's culture.

Assam

Assam is renowned for its handwoven silk, particularly Muga silk, and traditional crafts like bamboo work and Assam tea which define its cultural richness.

Chhattisgarh

Chhattisgarh is known for its Dhokra metal casting and Bastar art, unique handmade crafts that showcase tribal culture and craftsmanship.

Karnataka

Karnataka boasts intricate Channapatna toys and Bidriware crafts, along with its temples and art that speak to the state's deep cultural heritage.

Madhya Pradesh

Madhya Pradesh is home to Pithora paintings and Chanderi fabric, traditional art forms that are an integral part of its cultural legacy.

Manipur

Manipur is known for its classical Manipuri dance and vibrant handmade textiles that represent the state's rich cultural history.

Rajasthan

Rajasthan is famous for its traditional Bandhej fabric, puppetry, and exquisite miniature paintings that showcase the state's royal heritage.

Uttar Pradesh

Uttar Pradesh is renowned for its fine brassware and intricate carpet weaving, an expression of its deep-rooted artisanal culture.
